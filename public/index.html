Instructions:

  1. Click on the "click to begin!" button.
  2. Wait for page to load.
  3. If asked to enable camera, press allow.
  4. Choose between levels easy, medium, and hard.
  5. Easy level makes you do 1 pose to get a point, medium is 2 poses, hard is 3 poses
  6. When the image flashes, try to match the pose closely to earn points!
  7. Every time you complete a pose correctly, it disappears from the top pose list. If you complete the whole set, you get a point and then have a new set of poses to complete.
  8. In order to play with another person, send them the game link, have one person choose the level, and race one another to see who can get the most points in the 60 second round!
